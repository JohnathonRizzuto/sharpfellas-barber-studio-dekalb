About

The Sharpfellas Barber Studio, in DeKalb, IL

The Sharpfellas Barber Studio sits on E Lincoln Hwy in downtown DeKalb and runs like a private gentleman's club — but the door's open to anyone who wants the cut done right. Jay holds the main chair and built the shop's reputation by treating every appointment like the only one of the day. Cocktails-and-cigars vibe, fade-and-line precision.
